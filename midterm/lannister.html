Tywin Lannister eldest son of Tytos Lannister. Lord of Casterly Rock, Lord Paramount of the Westerlands, Warden of the West, Saviour of the city of the King’s Landing, Hand of the King and Protector of the realm (Killed by his son Tyrion) Joanna Lannister Wife of Tywin Lannister. Died after giving birth to Tyrion. She is also a first cousin of Tywin. Cersei Lannister Only daughter of Tywin and Joanna. Widow to the late King Robert Baratheon, Queen mother, Queen Dowager, former Queen Regent. At present Lady of Casterly Rock, Queen of the Andals and the first men and Protector of the seven Kingdoms. Jaime Lannister oldest son of Tywin and Joanna. Called as “The Kingslayer”. Former lord commander of the Kingsguard. Joffrey Baratheon Elder son of Jaime and Cersei. King of the Andals and the first men, Protector of the Realm and Lord of the seven Kingdoms. (Poisoned at his own wedding) Myrcella Baratheon Only daughter of Jaime and Cersei. Send for safety by Tyrion at Dorne. (Poisoned by Ellaria Sand) Tommen Baratheon Youngest son of Jaime and Cersei. Lord of the seven Kingdoms and King of the Andals and the first men. (Committed suicide after his wife’s death Margaery Tyrell) Tyrion Lannister Youngest son of Tywin and Joanna. Called as an “Imp” and “Halfman”, a dwarf, former Hand of the King and Master coin to King Joffrey. He is a fugitive after killing his father and condemned to death. Now Hand of the King to Daenerys Targaryen. Sansa Stark Wife to Tyrion Lannister by force. The marriage is still unconsummated. Now as a Lady of the Winterfell. Kevan Lannister Tywin’s younger brother and close advisor. Hand of the King to Tommen Baratheon. (Killed in the destruction of the great Sept of Baelor). Dorna Wife of Kevan Lannister. Lancel Lannister Eldest son of Kevan and Dorna. Former squire to King Robert Baratheon. Renamed his name as “Brother Lancel” after joining the Sparrows and the Faith Militant. (Killed during the destruction of the Sept of Baelor) Martyn Lannister Second son of Kevan and Dorna. He is a Squire in the Lannister army. (Murdered by Rickard Stark while hostage at Riverrun). Willem Lannister Youngest son of Kevan and Dorna A squire in the Lannister army. (Killed by Rickard Stark while hostage at Riverrun) Cadet Branches Alton Lannister He is a cousin to Cersei, Jaime and Tyrion. He comes from a lesser branch of this family. Alton remained as a prisoner to House Stark and get killed by Jaime as an escape. He states that his mother is Cynda Lannister. Lyman Lannister He is also a cousin to House Lannister from the main line. House Lannister of Lanisport This is a cadet branch of this family. Formally organise as a distant house. This house rules the city of Lanisport for the main branch. And the main branch rules the gold mine place of Casterly Rock in the nearby rocky mountains above Lanisport Reginald Lannister He is a bannerman of Tywin. He fought for House Lannister during the war of the five kings House Lannister is a wealthy, rich and powerful family more than 6000 years, dating back from the Age of heroes and Andal invasions. Due to this it has produced many cadet branch and distant relatives.
